Step 2: Fees and Prices for Amazon Sellers
Before selling your artwork on Amazon, you have to understand selling plans and fees to access Amazon tools and services. There are different types of fees associated with selling on Amazon.

Selling Art On Amazon Fee = Referral Fee + Closing Fee + Shipping Fee + Other Fee

Referral fees
For each artwork sold, a referral fee applies, which varies by product category. This fee is calculated as a percentage of the total selling price.

Closing Fees
The closing Fee is charged every time your art is sold on Amazon based on the price range of the art product. This fee also varies based on the fulfillment channel you are using.

Shipping Fees
Amazon has three selling methods for shipping your art to customers.

Easy Shipping
Self Shipping
Fulfillment by Amazon(FBA)
If you use Easy Ship or Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA), Amazon will deliver your artwork to the customer and charge you a fee. 

If you choose to self-ship, you will handle the cost of shipping and delivery of your artwork through 3rd party courier service or your own delivery agents.
